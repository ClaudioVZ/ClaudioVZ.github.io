Usando \( f(x) = 1 \) $$ \begin{equation*} a_{0} f(x_{0}) + a_{1} f(x_{1}) + a_{2} f(x_{2}) + a_{3} f(x_{3}) + a_{4} f(x_{4}) = \int_{x_{0}}^{x_{4}} 1 \ dx \end{equation*} $$
